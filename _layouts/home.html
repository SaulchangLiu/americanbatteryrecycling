Core Competencies

Small Electronic Parts Cost Reduction

Proven track record in analyzing component sourcing, optimizing designs, and negotiating supplier contracts to significantly reduce BOM costs without compromising quality or performance.

Battery Assembly

Expertise in precision assembly of battery packs across multiple chemistries (Li-ion, LiFePO4, etc.), ensuring safety, consistency, and scalability from prototype to production.

Battery Module Development

End-to-end capability from concept to production—including design, cell selection, BMS integration, mechanical housing, and thermal management—for custom and high-performance battery modules.

  • Experience supporting both commercial and government clients with flexible production volumes
  • Emphasis on reliability, compliance, and product lifecycle efficiency
